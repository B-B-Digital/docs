# What is a domain and why do I need one?

A domain is **your business's address on the internet** — for example `yourcompany.com`. It's exactly the same as a street address or a phone number: it's how customers find you.

## Why does it matter?

- A domain with your company's name builds trust and looks professional (`yourcompany.com` instead of a random free-service address)
- You own the domain — even if you switch website providers later, the domain stays yours
- An email address like `contact@yourcompany.com` also runs on your domain

## How much does it cost?

A domain is purchased for a year and renewed annually — a small, recurring cost (usually a modest yearly fee, depending on the ending, e.g. `.com`, `.pl`).

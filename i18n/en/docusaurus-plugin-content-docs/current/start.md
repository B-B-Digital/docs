# Welcome to the B&B Digital help center

This guide is written for people who are **ordering a website for the first time** and don't need to know anything about technology. We explain everything here, step by step, in plain language — no jargon, no complicated terms.

You don't need to memorize any of this. Our job is to take care of the technical side of the project for you — all we need from you is information about your business and a bit of patience.

# How does working with us look, step by step?

We keep the whole process as simple as possible for you. Here's how it goes:

## 1. Conversation and brief

We get to know your business, the goals for your site, and your expectations. We define the scope of the project.

## 2. Quote and agreement

We prepare a clear quote and timeline. Once you approve it, we sign the agreement and get to work.

## 3. Design

We prepare a visual proposal tailored to your brand — colors, layout, style.

## 4. Building the site

We build a working site based on the approved design, along with a domain, hosting, and an SSL certificate (see: [what is a domain](../faq/czym-jest-domena.md), [what is hosting and SSL](../faq/czym-jest-hosting-i-ssl.md)).

## 5. Your feedback and revisions

We show you the finished site before it goes live. You share feedback, and we make revisions.

## 6. Launch

The site goes live at its final address and becomes publicly available on the internet.

## 7. Support after launch

We stay available — helping with updates, questions, and small changes after launch.

# What should I prepare before the project starts?

To get things moving smoothly, it helps to have a few things ready. Don't worry if something's missing, though — we'll help you fill in the gaps.

## Good to have on hand

- **Your company logo** — if you already have one, ideally in good quality (e.g. a PNG or SVG file)
- **Text about your business** — this can be finished copy or just notes and bullet points that we'll develop together
- **Photos** — of your business, team, or past work; if you don't have any, we can suggest an alternative
- **Examples of sites you like** — even one or two links help us a lot in understanding your taste
- **Access to your domain** — if you already own a domain, share access with us; if not, we'll set one up for you

## What if something's missing?

That's completely normal. Many people start working with us without finished text or photos — we help fill in the missing pieces during the [conversation and brief](./jak-wyglada-wspolpraca.md) stage.

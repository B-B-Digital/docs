# How do I get access to the content panel?

Shortly after your site launches, an email from Sanity will land in your inbox — that's the name of the system behind your content panel. It might look like it's come from a company you've never heard of, but it's a standard part of the process, so please don't ignore it or let it slip into spam.

## The panel invite

The email contains a link inviting you to an account with the **Editor** role — meaning you can edit content on the site, but you don't have access to technical settings that could break something. Just click the link and set a password, or sign in right away with a Google account.

If more than one person at your business works on the site — say, you and your assistant at the practice — each of you will get a separate invite at your own email address.

## What we've already handled

Before that invite even reaches you, we've quietly added your domain to the list of addresses allowed to use the panel. It sounds technical, but in practice it means one thing: there's nothing for you to configure, install, or set up on your own. Our side of the work is already done — all that's left for you is to wait for the email and click the link.

If the invite hasn't arrived a few days after launch, check your spam folder first, then just drop us a line. Once you're in, move on to [How and where do I log in?](./logowanie-do-panelu.md)

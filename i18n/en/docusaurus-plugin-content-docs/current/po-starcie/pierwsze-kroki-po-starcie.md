# Your first steps after launch

Your site is already live, so the hardest part is behind us. Before you get back to your day-to-day business, we want to walk you through what happens over the next few days — so no email from us catches you off guard.

There's one task left: getting you set up in the panel where you edit your own content. It's a short process, but it happens in a few steps, each one needing a little of your attention.

## What happens, step by step

First we'll send you access, then you'll log in, and finally we'll confirm together that everything works as it should:

- **Access to the panel** — you'll get an email invite, see [How do I get access to the content panel?](./dostep-do-panelu.md)
- **Logging in** — we'll show you where and how to sign in, see [How and where do I log in?](./logowanie-do-panelu.md)
- **Your first change** — together we'll confirm publishing works, see [Your first change: how to confirm everything works](./pierwsza-publikacja.md)
- **What's next** — once you're comfortable in the panel, we'll show you what's worth doing next, see [What's next?](./co-dalej-po-starcie.md)

## How long it takes

Realistically, the whole process takes maybe fifteen minutes of your time, spread across an invite email and one short login. There's nothing to prepare in advance — just keep an eye on the inbox we agreed on when signing the contract.

Start by checking that inbox: [How do I get access to the content panel?](./dostep-do-panelu.md)

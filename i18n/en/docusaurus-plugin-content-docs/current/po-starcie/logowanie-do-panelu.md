# How and where do I log in?

You've got access now — the question is where to actually type in your password. For most of our clients the answer is simple, though there's one exception worth knowing about.

## The standard address

You'll find the content panel at your own domain, with `/admin` added at the end. If your site is `dental-clinic.pl`, for example, the panel will be at `dental-clinic.pl/admin`. There's nothing extra to remember — it's the same address your customers use to find your site, just with one extra word on the end.

## The exception: older hosting

Some sites — usually the ones built on older, traditional cPanel-style hosting — work a little differently. In those cases the panel lives at a separate address ending in `.sanity.studio`, instead of at your own domain. That's not a glitch or a workaround; it's simply a different way of connecting your site to the panel, depending on how your hosting is set up.

You don't need to figure out which version applies to you — we'll tell you plainly before you even click the first link in your invite, and the correct address will also be in our welcome email.

## Logging in

Whichever address applies, the login itself works the same way: you enter the email and password you set up during the invite (or sign in with Google) and land straight in the panel.

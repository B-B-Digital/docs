# What's next?

If you've made it this far, you already have access to the panel, know where to log in, and have confirmed that publishing changes actually works. The natural question now is: what's next?

## Learning your way around the panel

Everything we've shown so far covered a single text field. In practice, your site is made up of many sections — your services, your team, client reviews, maybe a blog — and it helps to know where each one lives and how to manage it. We've put that together in a separate guide: [How do I find my way around the panel?](../cms/jak-poruszac-sie-po-panelu.md)

## When you need more than editing

Not everything can, or should, be changed on your own in the panel. Changing the site's layout, adding a new feature, or getting help with something that looks suspicious — that falls under our technical support, not the content panel. Check [what's included in our post-launch support](../wsparcie/co-wchodzi-w-opieke.md) so you know when to reach out to us instead of trying to figure it out on your own.

You don't need to memorize any of this — this guide will always be here when you forget how to do something.

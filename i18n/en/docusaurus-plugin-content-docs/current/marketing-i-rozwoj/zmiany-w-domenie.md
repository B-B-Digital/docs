# Domain changes after launch

The domain we registered when your project launched isn't a one-time setting you're stuck with. As a business grows, there's often a need for a new email address, an extra page under a different address, or some other technical DNS change — and all of that is possible without moving your site or starting over.

## What usually changes

- **new email accounts** — for example, when a dental practice adds a second dentist who needs an address like reception@yourcompany.com alongside the existing contact@yourcompany.com
- **a subdomain** — a separate address like blog.lawfirm.com or booking.yourcoach.com, often for a new project or an integration with an external tool
- **other DNS records** — technical settings needed to, say, connect a newsletter tool, verify your domain with another service, or redirect to an additional address

## Who sets this up

We make these changes. The domain management panel sits on our technical side, so you don't need to dig into it yourself — just tell us what you need, and we'll work out the best way to configure it without affecting how your site or your email works.

Small changes, like a new email account, usually take a few minutes. Subdomains and more involved settings can need a bit more planning, especially if they need to work together with an external tool.

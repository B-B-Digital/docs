# Google Analytics and Search Console

These are two free Google tools we ask almost every client about at the start of a project, because they answer two very different questions: how many people visit your site and where they come from, and whether people can find you in search results at all.

## Google Analytics — who visits your site

Analytics collects traffic statistics: how many people visited, which pages they looked at most, and whether they arrived from Google, from Facebook, or by typing your address directly into the browser. For a law firm, that might mean finding out whether more visitors land on the services page or on a blog post with legal tips — which hints at where it's worth investing further.

## Search Console — how Google sees your site

Search Console shows the other side of it: whether Google has actually indexed your pages, which search terms you show up for, and whether there are any technical issues holding back your visibility. It's the tool that lets a dental practice check whether it actually appears in results for "dentist + city name."

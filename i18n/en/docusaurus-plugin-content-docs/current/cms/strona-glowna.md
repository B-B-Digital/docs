# How do I edit my homepage

Before anyone reads a word about what you offer, they'll see two sentences at the very top of your homepage — a large heading and the line underneath it. That's your first impression in its purest form, so it's worth making it specific: what you do and for whom, not just something that sounds nice.

## Heading and subheading

The large heading is a **required field** — without it, the homepage won't work properly, so Studio won't let you save the document until it's filled in. The subheading underneath is optional, but in practice it's almost always worth adding — it's your spot for one or two sentences that expand on the promise in the heading. A dentist might mention no waiting lists and painless treatment, a lawyer that the first consultation is free, a coach exactly which problem they help solve.

## Featured services

Further down the homepage, you can showcase up to six services from your offer — this is simply a selection from a list you maintain elsewhere (see [how do I add or edit a service](./usluga.md)). You're not writing names or descriptions again here — you're just picking which existing services should appear near the top of the homepage, and in what order. If you edit a service's description in its own document, the change appears here automatically too.

Keep in mind this is meant to be a selection, not the full list — the homepage only shows your most important offerings. Your complete price list and all your services still live on their own dedicated services page.

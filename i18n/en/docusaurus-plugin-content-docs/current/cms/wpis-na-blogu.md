# How do I add a blog post

**This guide only applies if your site has a blog section** — not every site we build includes one. If you're not sure whether yours does, check the menu on the left side of the panel: if you see "Blog Posts" there, this guide is for you.

A blog is a good place to show off the expertise you work with every day — a lawyer can explain what to watch for in a lease agreement, a dentist can clarify the difference between a crown and a veneer. It builds trust before anyone even calls, and it helps your site show up in search results along the way.

## Title and publish date

The post title also generates the address of its own page, the same way it does for services. The publish date determines the order posts appear in — the newest ones show up first, so if you're writing a post ahead of time, you can set the publish date in the future.

## Excerpt

The excerpt is a short summary shown in the post listing before anyone clicks into the full article — one or two sentences meant to draw someone in, not the first paragraph copied from the body.

## Cover image

Every post can have a cover image, shown in the post listing and at the top of the article itself. Uploading works the same way as any other photo in the panel ([how do I upload and crop photos](./jak-wgrywac-i-kadrowac-zdjecia.md)).

## Post body

You write the body itself in the rich text editor ([how do I use the rich text editor](./jak-korzystac-z-edytora-tekstu.md)) — with headings, bold text, quotes. Within the body you can also insert additional images anywhere you like, along with a caption underneath — handy for things like "before and after" photos or documenting a process step by step.

## SEO

Blog posts have their own SEO tab, just like services — covered in [how do I fill in the SEO tab](./jak-wypelnic-zakladke-seo.md).

If your site also has a news section, see [how do I add a news item](./aktualnosc.md) — usually a quicker, simpler format than a full blog post.

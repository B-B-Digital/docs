# How do I add or edit a team member

Before booking a visit or signing an agreement, people often want to see the face of the person they'll be dealing with. The team section covers exactly that — and, like services, it's a list of entries, so you add each new team member as a separate entry on that list.

## Name and job title

The two basic fields are the person's name and their job title or role — "Dr. Anna Kowalska, DDS", "Attorney", "Business coach". These appear directly alongside that person's photo.

## Portrait photo

A team member's photo works best in a vertical, portrait layout — framed from the chest up, rather than a landscape shot or a group photo. Uploading and cropping work the same way as any other photo in the panel ([how do I upload and crop photos](./jak-wgrywac-i-kadrowac-zdjecia.md)).

## Biography

The biography is a short piece of text about that person's experience, education, or specialization — you write it in the same rich text editor used for other longer content in the panel ([how do I use the rich text editor](./jak-korzystac-z-edytora-tekstu.md)). Unlike services, team members **don't get their own page or web address** — the entire biography lives on a single card, usually on the About page.

## Display order

Just like with services, the number in the "Display Order" field decides who appears first on the list — a lower number means a higher position. The owner of a practice or firm usually places themselves first, but that's entirely your call.

# How do I navigate the panel?

The first time you [log in to the panel](../po-starcie/logowanie-do-panelu.md), you'll see a list of items on the left that, at first glance, can blur into one long list. There's actually a simple logic behind it, and once it clicks, it stays clicked.

## The top of the list: pages you only have once

Right at the top of the menu, before any divider, you'll find the items that correspond to the fixed parts of your site — the ones that only ever exist as a single copy. In a typical setup, that's:

- Site Settings — company name, logo, general details
- Homepage — heading, featured services
- About page — the introduction to your practice or office
- Services Page — visibility and SEO settings for the services list
- Contact Info — address, phone, email, opening hours

Clicking any of these opens its editing form immediately. There's no in-between step like "pick which document" — in this group, each one only exists once.

## Below the line: lists you build up over time

Below a clear dividing line, the second part of the menu begins — items behind which there isn't a single document, but a whole collection. Here you'll typically find Service and Team Member, and if your site uses them, also Blog Post, News Item, and Testimonial.

Clicking one of these doesn't open an editing form right away — it opens a list of every existing entry of that type. From there you pick a specific entry to edit, or add a new one using the button in the top-right corner.

## Your menu might look a little different

Exactly what appears in the lower part of the menu depends on which features we've switched on for your site. A dental practice without a blog simply won't see "Blog Post" in the menu — and that's entirely normal. If you're expecting an item and it's not there, it's almost never a glitch; that feature just hasn't been switched on for you yet. A quick message to us sorts it out.

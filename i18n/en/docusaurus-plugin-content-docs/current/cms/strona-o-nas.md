# How do I edit my About page

The About page is often the moment a potential client decides whether to trust you. For a dental practice, a law firm, or a coaching business, it's one of the most visited pages — people want to know who they'll be dealing with before they even pick up the phone.

## Page subheading

The "Page Subheading" field is a short line shown under the "About" title — if you leave it blank, the page automatically falls back to the default tagline set for the whole site, so nothing breaks if you don't have a separate line in mind here.

## Introducing your practice

The main block of text on this page is where you tell the story of your business — your mission, how the practice came to be, your approach to clients. You write it in the rich text editor, exactly the same way as everywhere else in the panel — with bold text, headings, or a quote if they fit ([how do I use the rich text editor](./jak-korzystac-z-edytora-tekstu.md)). This field is optional, but a lawyer writing two sentences about why they started their own practice builds more trust than a blank page.

## Your team appears automatically

Right below the introduction, the page shows your team — but you add and edit those people in a separate part of the panel, **not here** (see [how do I add or edit a team member](./czlonek-zespolu.md)). Fill in your team list once, and the About page displays it on its own.

## SEO

This page also has its own SEO tab — the rules are the same as everywhere else, covered in [how do I fill in the SEO tab](./jak-wypelnic-zakladke-seo.md).

Now that you know how to tell your story, add the people behind it: [how do I add or edit a team member](./czlonek-zespolu.md).

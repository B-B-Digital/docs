# How do I add or edit a service

Services are usually the part of the site that decides whether a client picks up the phone. Unlike pages such as About or the homepage, you typically have several or a dozen services — which is why they appear in the panel as a list of entries rather than a single document (more on that difference in [one page or a list of entries](./jedna-strona-czy-lista-wpisow.md)).

## Name and short description

The service name doubles as both the card title on the site and the basis for its own page address — more on that below. The short description is one or two sentences shown on the service card before a client even clicks into it: this is the place for the essentials, not the full rundown of a treatment or a legal service.

## Photo and icon

Every service can have its own photo — uploading and cropping work the same way as everywhere else in the panel ([how do I upload and crop photos](./jak-wgrywac-i-kadrowac-zdjecia.md)). Besides the photo, there's also a field for an icon — but here you're not uploading a file, you're typing a single emoji, like 🦷 for a dental service or ⚖️ for a legal case. That emoji usually shows up on the service card as a light decorative touch.

## Full service description

The full description is the place for everything that didn't fit in the short one — how a treatment proceeds, what a service covers, who it's meant for. You write it in the rich text editor ([how do I use the rich text editor](./jak-korzystac-z-edytora-tekstu.md)), and it's displayed on that service's own dedicated page — the one a client lands on after clicking the card.

That page address — for example `yourcompany.com/services/teeth-whitening` — **is created automatically from the service name**. You don't need to set anything manually.

## Display order

The "Display Order" field is a plain number — the lower it is, the higher up (or further left, depending on the layout) the service appears in the list. If you have ten services and want your most profitable or most requested one to appear first, this is where you set it.

# Is my content backed up?

Short answer: yes. And you don't have to do anything about it — it's one of those things we quietly take care of in the background, so you never have to think about it.

## Who takes care of it

We back up your site and its content regularly and automatically, as part of our ongoing support. You don't need to export anything, save files to your own computer, or keep track of when it last happened — it's on us, the same way hosting or the SSL certificate is.

## What this means for you in practice

Picture this: you run a coaching practice, and you're editing the description of one of your programs in the content panel. Something goes wrong — you accidentally delete half the text right before you're about to send the link to a potential client. Instead of rewriting everything from scratch, just let us know — we pull up the earlier version and restore what disappeared.

The same goes for more serious, though far rarer, situations: a server outage or a glitch during a software update. Your content doesn't vanish along with it, because we always have something to restore it from.

The one thing worth keeping track of yourself is anything that exists only on your end and never made it onto the site — original high-resolution photos, for instance, or internal company documents. Anything that's already made it onto the site is covered by our backups.

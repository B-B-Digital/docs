# What's included in ongoing support, and what's billed separately

Support for your site doesn't end the day it goes live. We stay by your side for as long as we work together, but it's worth knowing exactly where our standard support ends and separately quoted work begins.

## What's included in support

Keeping your site running covers a handful of things you never have to think about:

- **Hosting and the domain** — your site keeps running on the server we manage
- **The SSL certificate** — we make sure it's always current, so visitors see a padlock next to your address instead of a browser warning
- **Security updates** — we keep the software your site runs on up to date, before anyone gets a chance to exploit its weak spots
- **Bug fixes** — if something that used to work stops working, we fix it at no extra cost (see [Bug or new feature?](./blad-czy-nowa-funkcja.md) for exactly what that covers)

In other words: everything that keeps the site alive and working exactly as it did on launch day is on us, and none of it costs you anything.

## What's billed separately

We quote separately for anything that goes beyond the site's original scope — that's a change, not a repair. Say you run a dental practice and want to add a new page describing services that were never part of your original offer — that's a separate job. Or you run a coaching practice and would love a new language version of the site, or a completely different homepage layout — that also goes beyond standard support.

This category typically includes:

- new features and pages that weren't part of the original project
- redesigning the look of the site, or major parts of it
- restructuring your content, for example changing how your services are presented

None of this is a problem — we simply treat it as a small new project, one we quote before starting, just like we did the first time around.
